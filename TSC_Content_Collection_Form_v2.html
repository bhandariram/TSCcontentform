What We'll Ask For
Content Checklist
Please review this list so you can prepare your materials before filling out the form. It only takes about 15-20 minutes.
🏢

Organisation Info

Required
  • Full official organisation name
  • Tagline / slogan
  • Year established
  • Registration / incorporation number
  • Office address in Ontario
  • Contact email & phone number
  • Social media links (Facebook, etc.)
📄

About Us Content

Required
  • About the Society (2-3 paragraphs)
  • Mission statement
  • Vision statement
  • History / founding story
  • Key achievements so far
  • What makes TSC unique?
👥

Team & Leadership

Required
  • President's name, title & photo
  • Executive committee members (names, titles, photos)
  • Brief bio for each leader (2-3 sentences)
  • Organisational structure info
🎫

Photos & Media

Required
  • High-resolution logo (PNG, SVG)
  • 10-20 event photos (high quality)
  • Community gathering photos
  • Cultural festival photos
  • Hero banner image for homepage
  • Any videos (YouTube links)
📅

Events & Activities

Required
  • List of upcoming events (title, date, location, description)
  • Recurring annual events list
  • Past event highlights (3-5 events with description)
  • Activities & programs offered
💳

Membership Details

Required
  • Membership fee amount (in CAD)
  • Membership categories (Individual, Family, etc.)
  • Benefits of membership (list)
  • Membership terms & conditions
  • What info to collect from applicants?
💰

Payment Information

Required
  • Bank account details for receiving payments
  • Currency confirmation (CAD)
  • Receipt / invoice requirements
📜

Certificate Details

Required
  • Certificate signatory name & title
  • Signatory's signature image (PNG, transparent bg)
  • Organisation seal / stamp image
  • Certificate wording / text you'd like
  • Membership ID format preference
🖊

Page-Specific Content

If Available
  • Homepage welcome message
  • Homepage statistics (member count, events/year, chapters, years active)
  • Contact page — Google Maps address to embed
  • Contact page — WhatsApp number for chat button
  • FAQ list (common questions & answers)
  • Gallery photo captions / descriptions
  • Any legal text (privacy policy, terms of use)
  • Any specific design preferences or reference websites
📧

Email & Social Media

Required
  • Existing email addresses to display on website
  • Facebook page link
  • Instagram profile link (if any)
  • YouTube channel link (if any)
  • TikTok or other social media links (if any)
